- An interest of mine for a while has been prose, whether that be fictional or relating to philosophy. Like I've mentioned, thinking and creating is what I most enjoy and language serves as an incredibly effecient medium for expressing this thinking. 

- I've mainly engaged in the medium in more of a utilitarian sense, using it to just describe my thinking. As of late, I've been reading more and more (fiction and philosophy) and become very compelled by the nature of language itself. This marks the beginning of my journey in consuming and mastering this craft.
